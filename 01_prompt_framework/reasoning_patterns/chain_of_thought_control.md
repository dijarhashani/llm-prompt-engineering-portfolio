# Chain-of-Thought Control

This pattern manages **how and when reasoning occurs**.

Uncontrolled reasoning increases hallucination risk.

---

## Strategies
- Explicit reasoning steps
- Hidden reasoning with summarized output
- Step-limited reasoning

---

## Example

Think step by step internally.
Provide only the final structured answer.

---

## Use Cases
- High-risk domains
- Regulated outputs
- Cost-sensitive systems

---

## Anti-Patterns
- Always-on chain-of-thought
- Exposing raw reasoning unnecessarily
